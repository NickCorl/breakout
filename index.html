Breakout 2.0

Instructions:

  1. Use the mouse to the control the paddle. By moving left or right over the canvas.
  2. Use the keyboard to navigate through game menus. The in-game menu will prompt for a ceartian key to press

Goal:

Move the paddle to bounce the ball to collide with all the blocks on screen. Breaking all blocks means you 'broke out' and you will allow you to progress to the next level. With each level a new row of blocks appear making the game harder and harder as you progress. If the ball leaves the bottom edge of the screen you will lose a life! And you only get 3!

Have fun and keep trying to beat the highscore!
